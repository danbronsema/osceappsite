#FOAMed

osceApp supports #FOAMed (Free Open Access Meducation). There's so much great medical information out there, but too much of it is behind paywalls, within private hospital networks, and sitting on decaying hard-drives. Help add to the wealth of free accessible medical knowledge. Join the conversation on twitter: #FOAMed.
